Welcome to Clear Mind – Mental Health Awareness Campaign

At Batangas Eastern Colleges (BEC), we recognize that mental health is just as important as physical well-being. College life comes with challenges—academic pressures, personal struggles, and the need to balance multiple responsibilities. That’s why we launched this Mental Health Awareness Campaign—to create a safe space where students can find support, learn coping strategies, and break the stigma surrounding mental health.

Through informative resources, interactive activities, and peer support initiatives, we aim to empower students to take care of their mental well-being. Whether you're looking for stress management tips, professional guidance, or simply a community that listens, we are here for you.

Join us in fostering a healthier, more supportive college environment. Let’s start the conversation and take a step toward better mental health—together.
